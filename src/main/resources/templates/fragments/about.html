Kat's been fascinated with computers, technology, and science since she was a young child.
She learned most of what she knows about computers and coding from exploring and experimenting on her own, reading technical documentation and forums, going through guides and tutorials, troubleshooting, problem-solving, critical thinking, and tons of trial-and-error.
She's an extremely kinesthetic and visual learner. She learns best from getting hands-on experience, tackling new challenges, working on real-world projects, seeing visual data, and understanding the "why?" of things.
She has an eye for detail and presentation, making her an ideal candidate for UI/UX design. She envisions how users will interact with with an interface and designs it to be user-friendly and aesthetically appealing.
She also loves logic, efficiency, and organization, making her an ideal candidate for logical programming and process optimization.
